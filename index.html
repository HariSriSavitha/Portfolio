INDIAN DEFENCE SERVICES

ARMY

The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Army, and it is commanded by the Chief of Army Staff, who is a four-star general.

NAVY

The Indian Navy is the naval branch of the Indian Armed Forces , principally designated for naval and amphibious warfare;namely riverine,littoral, or ocean-borne combat operations and related functions.The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Navy.The Chief of Naval Staff, a four-star admiral, commands the navy.
More Details

AIR FORCE

The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. Its complement of personnel and aircraft assets ranks fourth amongst the air forces of the world.Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and to conduct aerial warfare during armed conflict.
